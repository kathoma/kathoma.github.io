ABOUT

Kevin Thomas

Kevin is a co-founder of Saliency, a Silicon Valley-based startup that developed AI-enabled medical imaging biomarkers. Saliency was acquired by Clario in 2020 and he now serves as their Director of AI. He is also a researcher within the Stanford University Neuromuscular Biomechanics Lab where he leverages AI to further the understanding of musculoskeletal diseases. Kevin has worked with some of the top organizations in health and human performance (Adidas, Philadelphia Phillies, EXOS) to advance their machine learning and biomechanics capabilities. He has a PhD in biomedical informatics from Stanford University and completed the first two years of medical school at Stanford School of Medicine. Before attending graduate school, he conducted research in Canada as a Fulbright Scholar.
